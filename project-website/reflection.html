Team Work Record

What went well

1. All work were done timely.

2. Team members can deliver assigned work on time.

3. Team members without a design background now know what a “design thinking” approach is.



Lessons

1. It’s never too much to pay attention to communication.

2. It’s better to have an early start rather than working over night to catch the deadline.

3. Prepare well before having a meeting, to make the meeting more productive.

4. Review the teamwork-deliverable before making submission, to make sure the overall consistency in both content and quality.



If It Starts Over Again

1. Will pay attention to build a team culture.

2. Have a better planning for the time.

3. Empower the teammates.

4. Sync necessary information to keep the team aligned.
